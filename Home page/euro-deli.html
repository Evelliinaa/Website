Eastern Cuisine - Euro Deli

Eastern European cuisine is known for its simple, seasonal ingredients like potatoes, cabbage, beets, and grains such as barley and buckwheat.

Pickling is a key technique, along with dairy fermentation, as seen in kefir. The cuisine has remained consistent for centuries due to the region's limited ingredients and climate.

Popular dishes include goulash, pierogi, and borscht. Common flavorings include dill, fatty sour cream, and fresh chives.
